The AI That Sees & Controls Your PC

IRA is an autonomous desktop AI agent running locally on your computer. Powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash & Gemini Live API, she reads your screen in real-time, executes complex keyboard & mouse actions, automates browser DOM, and remembers your workflow.
