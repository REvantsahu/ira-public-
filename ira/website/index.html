◈ Process

How IRA Thinks

IRA doesn't just follow commands — she sees, reasons, and acts. Like a human, but faster.

See

Takes a screenshot and analyzes what's on your screen. Identifies apps, buttons, text, and context.

Identify

Finds interactive elements — buttons, inputs, menus. Uses DOM-level precision for web, vision for desktop.

Reason

Decides the best action. Which button to click? What text to type? Plans the optimal path to your goal.

Act

Executes the action — clicks, types, scrolls. Uses the right tool for the job, every time.
